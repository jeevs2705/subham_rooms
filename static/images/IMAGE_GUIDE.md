# Image Upload Guide for Subham Rooms

## Required Images

### Main Room Images (shown on home page):
1. `room-small4.jpg` - Small Room for 4 People
2. `room-small2.jpg` - Mini Room for 2 People
3. `room-big8.jpg` - Big Room for 8 People
4. `restroom.jpg` - Restroom/Bathroom

### Additional Room Detail Images (shown on room detail pages):
For each room, you can add up to 3 images:

**Small Room (4 People):**
- `room-small4.jpg` (main image)
- `room-small4-2.jpg` (second view)
- `room-small4-3.jpg` (third view)

**Mini Room (2 People):**
- `room-small2.jpg` (main image)
- `room-small2-2.jpg` (second view)
- `room-small2-3.jpg` (third view)

**Big Room (8 People):**
- `room-big8.jpg` (main image)
- `room-big8-2.jpg` (second view)
- `room-big8-3.jpg` (third view)

## How to Add Images:

1. Take photos of your rooms from different angles
2. Rename them according to the names above
3. Copy all images to: `static/images/` folder
4. Refresh your website

## Image Tips:

- Use good lighting when taking photos
- Show the room from different angles
- Capture the bed, windows, and overall space
- Keep images under 2MB each
- Recommended size: 800x600 pixels

If images are not uploaded, placeholder emojis will be shown.
